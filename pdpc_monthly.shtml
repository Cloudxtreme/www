This form can be used in order to set up a monthly, automatic donation to Peer-Directed Projects Center (PDPC) via a PayPal subscription. Donations to PDPC are unconditional and irrevocable and will be used to fund PDPC programs and general operating expenses.

Donations via PayPal subscription are provided as a convenience to PDPC donors. You must belong to PayPal to use this option. Your subscription is made or cancelled through PayPal and the fact that you've set up such a subscription is not considered by PDPC to be a pledge or other promise of a future donation.

To begin the donation process, follow these steps:

  1. Select a support level from the drop-down box.
  2. If you're a freenode user, and you'd like to wear the Cloak, we appreciate it! Just set up your IRC nick and enter your main nickname in the space provided in the form. Your acknowledgement cloak will appear on your IRC sessions until you discontinue your subscription.
  3. If you spent much of your freenode time on a single channel, and you'd like to list your donations as coming from that channel, just enter its name in the bottom box. We'll keep a running tally of the number of users contributing from each channel.
  4. Hit Submit to begin the process.

That's all it takes. On behalf of the board of Peer-Directed Projects Center, thank you for your contribution!
