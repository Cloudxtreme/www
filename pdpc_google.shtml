The above button can be used to make a one-off donation to Peer-Directed Projects Center (PDPC) via Google Checkout. Donations to PDPC are unconditional and irrevocable and will be used to fund PDPC programs and general operating expenses.

Donations via Google Checkout are provided as a convenience to PDPC donors.

To begin the donation process, follow these steps:

  1. Select a support level from the drop-down box.
  2. If you're a freenode user, and you'd like to wear the Cloak, we appreciate it! Just set up your IRC nick and contact a freenode staffer after making your donation. Staff can be found in #freenode where all staff members are voiced. You will need to have the details of your donation at hand.Your acknowledgement cloak will appear on your IRC sessions for a year from your most recent donation.

That's all it takes. On behalf of the board of Peer-Directed Projects Center, thank you for your contribution!
