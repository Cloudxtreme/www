freenode's next generation ircd project, ircd-seven, is a branch of the charybdis ircd, with changes specific to freenode's needs. It has been designed to replace the legacy hyperion. The network was migrated to ircd-seven at the end of January, 2010.

The source code, as well as a wiki and bug tracker are available in Redmine. If you're interested in learning more about ircd-seven, consult the Redmine and join us in #freenode-seven.
