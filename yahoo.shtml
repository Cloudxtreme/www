We are proud to announce that after long running talks between yahoo executives and the pdpc steering committee, Yahoo has agreed to buy pdpc and the freenode irc network.

This purchase will improve the availability of freenode through the use of multiple redundant backup servers in secured yahoo facilities. In addition, yahoo plans to leverage the experience and know-how we posses to greatly improve yahoo chat and related technologies.

Keep an eye on the blog for further updates!
