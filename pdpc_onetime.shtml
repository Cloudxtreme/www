This form can be used in order to make a single, one-time donation to Peer-Directed Projects Center, the not-for-profit entity which runs freenode. Donations are unconditional and irrevocable and will be used to fund PDPC programs and general operating expenses.

Donations are accepted through PayPal, but you will not be required to join PayPal in order to make a one-time donation. To donate without joining, simply use a debit or credit card which has never been associated with a PayPal account, and click the button labeled "If you have never paid through PayPal, click here."

If you'd like to make an ongoing contribution via PayPal, click here instead. An automatic, once-a-year charge/debit will be set up which you can cancel at any time. You must belong to PayPal to use this option.

To begin the donation process, follow these steps:
  1. Select a support level from the drop-down box.

  2. If you're a freenode user, and you'd like to wear the Cloak, we appreciate it! Just set up your IRC nick and enter your main nickname in the space provided in the form. Your acknowledgement cloak will appear on your IRC sessions for a year. But, even if you donate at the smaller nibble or byte levels, we'd love it if you could let us know your nick. We'll be happy to provide a cloak, on request, as your yearly total reaches one of the higher levels.

  3. If you spent much of your freenode time on a single channel, and you'd like to list your donation as coming from that channel, just enter its name in the bottom box. We'll keep a running tally of the number of users contributing from each channel.

  4. Hit Submit to begin the process.

That's all it takes. On behalf of the board of Peer-Directed Projects Center, thank you for your contribution!
